## Purpose
Analyse a business decision, opportunity, or problem with structured thinking and a clear recommendation.

## Load Skills
> Before proceeding, read and apply in order:
> 1. `skills/analysis/SKILL.md` — for the assessment
> 2. `skills/planning/SKILL.md` — for the recommendation and next steps

## Prompt

```
Analyse the following situation and provide a strategic recommendation.

Situation: [DESCRIBE THE DECISION, PROBLEM, OR OPPORTUNITY]
Context: [RELEVANT BACKGROUND — COMPANY SIZE, INDUSTRY, CURRENT STATE]
Options being considered: [LIST OPTIONS IF KNOWN, OR "OPEN" IF NOT]
Constraints: [BUDGET, TIME, RESOURCES, NON-NEGOTIABLES]
Decision maker: [WHO IS THIS FOR AND WHAT DO THEY CARE ABOUT MOST]
Recommendation needed: [yes / no — just assessment]
```

## Usage Notes
- The more context you provide, the sharper the analysis
- If you have data (financials, metrics), paste it after the prompt
- Use "Recommendation needed: no" when you want assessment without being pushed toward a conclusion
