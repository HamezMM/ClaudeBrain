## Purpose
Prepare for an important meeting with a clear agenda, goals, and talking points.

## Load Skills
> Before proceeding, read and apply: `skills/planning/SKILL.md`

## Prompt

```
Help me prepare for the following meeting.

Meeting type: [1:1 / team / client / negotiation / presentation / other]
With: [WHO IS IN THE ROOM AND THEIR ROLES]
Goal: [WHAT DOES SUCCESS LOOK LIKE FOR THIS MEETING]
Duration: [HOW LONG]
My role: [AM I LEADING, PRESENTING, NEGOTIATING, UPDATING]
Key topics to cover: [LIST]
Concerns or sensitivities: [ANYTHING I'M WORRIED ABOUT OR NEEDS CAREFUL HANDLING]
```

## Usage Notes
- For difficult meetings (performance conversations, negotiations), the "Concerns" field is the most important one
- Output includes agenda, talking points, and anticipated questions/objections
- Ask Claude to roleplay the other party if you want to practice responses
